6. https://sddesignweek.org/ I like how everything moves with the cursor. As you scroll down more information pops up, so it's clearer for the eye and not everything at once. 7. https://bradstarnes.com/ As a website designer by profession, I think Starnes knows how to write out and use motion to a great experience on the "canvas" or screen. 8. https://www.turnstl.com/ I think it has really great engagement right off the bat with the moving animation lines. Then it settles into into large dynamic fonts and simple color blocks that captures the eye. It has really simple buttons and I like the way each paragraph and photo has a bit of tottering movement before it settles into static for clearer viewing.
